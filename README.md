## About Paralives

Paralives is a **life simulation** built around **Parafolks** — fully customizable characters you create in **Paramaker**, place in homes you design in **Build Mode**, and direct in **Live Mode** across an open town. The game emphasizes creative building (including curved walls, split levels, and a flexible color system), deep character identity (talents, vibes, stats, emotions, and relationships), and fair monetization: **no paid DLC** is planned; updates and modding support are part of the core vision.

Early Access is expected to deliver a playable loop: create Parafolks, furnish lots, manage needs and wants, pursue skills and careers, socialize, and expand content over time through patches and community mods via **Steam Workshop**.

## Key features (at a glance)

| Area | What players get |
|------|------------------|
| **Paramaker** | Create Parafolks with looks, talents, vibes, personality points, and outfits before you play. |
| **Build Mode** | Grid-less placement, curved architecture, interiors, and strong color control for homes and venues. |
| **Live Mode** | Direct Parafolks in an open town — needs, wants, emotions, skills, jobs, shops, and social systems. |
| **Talents & vibes** | Long-term identity picks that shape skills, moods, and social flavor (not passed genetically to children). |
| **Relationships** | Social perks, Together Cards, and group dynamics that reward different playstyles. |
| **Economy & careers** | Bills, work, shopping, and venues such as the museum tied to the Early Access town. |
| **Modding** | In-game mod tools and Workshop support; the developer has stated a no paid-DLC approach. |
| **Early Access roadmap** | Day-one versus during-EA feature lists — see Updates and the Simulator feature filter on the hub. |

## Frequently asked questions

**When does Paralives release on Steam?**  
Steam Early Access is listed for **May 25, 2026**. Dates can shift — confirm on the [Steam page](https://store.steampowered.com/app/1118520/Paralives/) and our [Updates](https://paralives.wiki/updates) section before you plan time off.

**Is paralives.wiki official?**  
No. Paralives Hub is a **fan-maintained** guide site. It aggregates player-facing explanations, tools, and links; it does not replace developer support or store policies.

**What is the difference between Wiki and Guides?**  
The **Wiki** is reference material organized by game system (talents, build tools, economy, etc.). **Guides** are narrative walkthroughs focused on tasks and play sessions.

**Do I need the game to use the Simulator?**  
No. Simulator tools run in the browser at [paralives.wiki/simulator](https://paralives.wiki/simulator) for planning only.

**Will mods be available at launch?**  
Modding is a stated priority with Workshop support; exact day-one mod tooling should be verified in-game and on [Updates](https://paralives.wiki/updates) after release. See [Modding](https://paralives.wiki/wiki/modding) for background.

**Where can I wishlist or buy the game?**  
On [Steam](https://store.steampowered.com/app/1118520/Paralives/). The hub does not sell keys or handle refunds.

**How do I report a wiki error or suggest a guide?**  
Use [Contact Us](https://paralives.wiki/legal/contact-us) with the page URL and a short description. Major patch changes are best cross-checked with official channels after updates ship.
